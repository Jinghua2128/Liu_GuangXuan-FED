Go Global Festival GGF

Attending the Go Global Festival (GGF) at Ngee Ann Polytechnic was an exciting and meaningful experience for me. As someone who values cultural exchange, being a part of the event allowed me to learn about and celebrate the rich diversity within our community. Representing the China booth, I felt a sense of pride introducing visitors to aspects of my heritage, such as the game Black Myth: Wukong, traditional snacks like Latiao and La Pian, and my Hanfu. It was heartwarming to see attendees engaging with the booth, showing genuine interest in Chinese culture. The festival also gave me an opportunity to connect with students representing other countries, broadening my perspective and fostering a deeper appreciation for different traditions. Overall, the experience left me feeling inspired and more connected to the global community.
