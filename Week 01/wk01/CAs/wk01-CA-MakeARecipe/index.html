Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry

This Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry recipe is made with fresh veggies and delicious sauce made with honey, soy sauce, and toasted sesame oil! It takes 20 minutes to make and enjoy your meal afterwards.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch

Str Fry Sauce

  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 2 tbsp cold water
  • 1/4 cup low sodium chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tbsp vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides, about 8-10 minutes.
  3. Add the broccoli florets, carrots, and snap peas to the wok, stirring occasionally to combine.
  4. Add the soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil to the wok, stirring to combine.
  5. Stir in the cornstarch, cover the wok, and cook for 2-3 minutes, until the sauce thickens.
  6. Serve the stir-fry over steamed rice or cooked brown rice.
  7. Enjoy your Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry!
